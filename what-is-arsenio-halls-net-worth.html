    It’s been a while since legendary television host and comedian Arsenio Hall was in the spotlight.

    He never really faded from popularity, but alongside the explosive personalities of some of the world’s biggest stars, Hall has a tendency to maintain a low profile. Perhaps this is due to his age — the Coming to America star recently rang in 66 years — or perhaps it’s due simply to his personality.

    Regardless of the reason, Hall has mostly stayed below the radar in recent years, apart from the occasional big role or major television appearance. Hall has only appeared in a few roles in the years since big moments — like his 2012 Celebrity Apprentice win — helped to define his career. His latest role saw Hall return to a range of decades-old roles in 2021’s Coming 2 America, which he starred in alongside Eddie Murphy.

    His work as a comedian through the ’80s and ’90s in particular helped Hall accumulate a comfortable monetary cushion. Despite his lack of consistent, box office-breaking work in recent years, the 66-year-old actor and comedian will likely retire on tens of millions of dollars.

    Arsenio Hall’s net worth

    Years of appearing in comedy specials, followed by more than a decade of hosting television programs and appearing in bit roles across popular programs — almost always as himself — has allowed Hall to build up an impressive net worth. He’s appeared in less than five projects in the last handful of years, but his slowdown in work isn’t affecting his accumulated wealth.

    As of 2022, Hall is worth right around $30 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Much of this wealth was built through years of work on screen, but a solid portion was also accumulated following several wise real estate decisions. Hall made a number of brilliant real estate purchases throughout his life, including a truly mind-boggling steal in 1990. These helped to cushion his already impressive income and will likely lead to a thoroughly cushy retirement for the former Arsenio Hall Show host.
