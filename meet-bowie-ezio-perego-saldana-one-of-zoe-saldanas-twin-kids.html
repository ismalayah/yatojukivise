    Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana is one of the twin sons of Zoe Saldana, an American famous actress whom she had with her husband, Marco Perego. Bowie was born on 27th November 2014 and is presently 9 years old.

    Bowie’s mother, Zoe Saldana, is famous for her work in three highest-grossing science fiction film franchises, Avatar, Avatar: The Way of Water, and Avengers: Endgame. Since her rise to fame, actress Zoe’s life has remained a thing of interest to her fans. Apart from her professional life, people also want to know more about her personal life and family, hence, her son’s biography. Find out all about the celebrity child.

    Summary of Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana’s Bio

    • AGE: 9 Years Old
    • BIRTHDAY: 27th November 2014
    • BIRTHPLACE: Los Angeles, California, United States
    • BIRTH SIGN: Sagittarius
    • ETHNICITY: Mixed
    • FATHER: Marco Perego
    • MOTHER: Zoë Yadira Saldaña-Perego
    • SIBLINGS: 2 (a twin brother Cy Aridio and Zen)

    How Old is Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana?

    Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana is currently 9 years old. He who was born on the 27th day of November 2014 in Los Angeles, California, United States of America, and is one of the twin kids of Zoe Saldana. Bowie was born early alongside his twin brother, Cy Aridio, through an emergency C-section.

    Going by his birth date, Bowie’s star sign is Saggitarius and his family follows the Christian religion. He is an American national with a mixed ethnic background. While his father is Italian, his mother is originally from Passaic, New Jersey, but has Dominican and Puerto Rican ancestry. Actress Zoe divulged in an interview that she is 1/4 Puerto Rican and 3/4 Dominican.

    According to her, her children will be multi-lingual as she and her husband speak Italian, Spanish, and English around them. Information about the name of the school Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana currently attends is not public knowledge, but looking at his age, he will still be receiving his elementary education at the moment from a school around their home in Los Angeles.

    Bowie has no personal claim to fame but has remained in the limelight only because of his mother’s celebrity status. He is still concentrating on his education and is yet to choose or start a career for himself. However, considering how successful his parents have been on their individual career paths, it will not be surprising to see the champ growing up to follow in their footsteps and achieve success in his own right.

    Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana Has an Identical Twin and One Other Brother

    The name of Bowie’s twin brother is Cy Aridio Saldaña-Perego. Just like Bowie, he is also 9 years old. Cy was named after his late maternal grandpa, Aridio, and Cy Twombly, the late American painter, sculptor, and photographer. Bowie and Cy are inseparable as they do everything together, according to their mother.

    Bowie and Aridio also have another sibling; a boy named Zen. Zen is the youngest in the family and was born on 12th December 2016. He is casually referred to as a tiny Yougi baby. The three brothers are growing up together under the guidance and love of their parents, and from what is obvious, they do not lack any necessity a child could ever need.

    Who is Bowie’s Father?

    Bowie Ezio’s father is Marco Perego-Saldaña, an American-based Italian artist, film producer, and former soccer player. Marco was born on 1st March 1979, making him 44 years old at the moment. This means that his wife, Zoe, is almost a year older than him. Marco was born and raised in Salo, Brescia, Italy, and is the only child of his father, Ezio Perego. He is an Italian-American citizen of white ethnic background.

    Growing up, Marco Perego played soccer and was part of a Venice football club where he played professional football until he was 21 years old. However, he sustained a severe leg injury which forced him to quit the sport to pursue another career. Marco graduated from IULM University in Milan, Italy.

    Meanwhile, Bowie’s father had a passion for art as a child so after he sustained a leg injury, he quickly went back to his childhood interest and today, he is an artist and film producer. Although he became more famous following his marriage to the star actress, Marco Perego first gained prominence after renowned film producer Gilda Moratti purchased one of his art pieces.

    He started his art career in 2002 but it did not pick up quickly until Moratti bought one of his paintings after which his art gradually began to grow noticeably. Marco Perego-Saldaña is credited for painting the background for Dolce & Gabbana’s Where Fashion Meets Art campaign. His art is displayed in international galleries like Museo Tuscolano, Royal Arts Academy, Art Basel, and Zurich’s Galerie Gmurzynska.

    In addition to art, Bowie’s father is a film producer and takes the credit for producing such films as Black and White Stripes: The Juventus Story, Keyhole Garden, and Me + Her.

    Meet Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana’s Mother, Zoë Saldaña

    As earlier mentioned, Bowie came into the limelight thanks to his parents’ fame, especially his mother Zoe Saldaña. She was born Zoë Yadira Saldaña Nazario on 19th June 1978 and is currently 45 years old. Zoë started her career as a dancer and at the age of 17, she had her way into the Hollywood industry. Since then, she has seen success in her acting career and remained one of the most sought-after actresses in the industry.

    Although she has been acting a decade before then, Zoë Saldaña rose to massive fame in 2009 after she appeared in science fiction movies; Avatar, Star Trek, Avatar: The Way of Water, and Guardian of the Galaxy. With multiple movies and TV shows under her belt, Bowie’s mother has had a successful career in Hollywood and is a recipient of numerous prestigious awards.

    Bowie Ezio Perego-Saldana’s Parents Are Still Together and Have Been Married For 10 Years

    Bowie’s parents, Marco Perego and Zoe Saldana, met for the first time on a New York flight in March 2013. It was love at first sight and they felt a strong connection between them immediately. They dated for the next three months and got married in June 2013 in a private wedding ceremony attended by only close friends and family.

    The couple has been together since then, living happily and raising their children together. After their marriage, Perego adopted Zeo’s surname Saldaña and became Marco Perego-Saldaña while Zoe became Zoë Saldaña-Perego. Their children are Perego-Saldaña. The couple tries to keep their children considerably away from the limelight.

    They occasionally share their photos on their various social media accounts but do not reveal their personal information on the media. One of the core values they emphasize and inculcate in their sons is that fathers and mothers can be strong role models.
